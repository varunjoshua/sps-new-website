About the ESPON Range

All SPS products are sold under the ESPON brand — formulated and developed by Mr. S.P. Shenoy and his team of IIT-trained metallurgists since 1985. Each product addresses a specific, measurable problem in high-temperature metal forming, with proven results across hundreds of installations worldwide.
