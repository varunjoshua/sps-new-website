Titanium & Super-Alloys

Titanium · Inconel · Critical Aerospace Alloys

High-performance alloys such as Titanium and Inconel are used in aerospace, defence, and critical engineering applications. These materials are expensive and highly sensitive to oxidation at forging and heat treatment temperatures. Alpha-case formation and oxidation losses make protective coatings essential — not optional.
