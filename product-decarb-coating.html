ESPON-DC
Decarburisation Control for High-Carbon Steels
ESPON-DC is formulated to control the depth of decarburisation during heat treatment of high-carbon steel components. By limiting oxygen and carbon monoxide contact with the steel surface, it ensures that decarb depth stays within the tighter tolerances required for critical engineering applications where surface hardness uniformity is essential.

Key Benefits

Controls decarb depth to within specification
Prevents excess decarburisation in heat treatment
Uniform hardness across treated component surface
Reduces rejection rate in QC hardness testing
Used in leaf spring and railway rail production
Prevents quench cracking caused by deep decarb layers
