SPS SPRAY SYSTEM
Complete Die Lubrication Spray System
The SPS Die Lubricant Spray System is a complete, configurable solution for die lubrication in forging operations. It replaces manual hand-spray application with a consistent, automated or semi-automated system that delivers the correct lubricant volume at the correct pressure to every section of the die face on every stroke cycle — eliminating variation in lubricant application that causes die life inconsistency.

Key Benefits

Consistent lubricant delivery volume on every stroke
Configurable nozzle layout for any die geometry
Eliminates manual spray variation and human error
Optimises lubricant consumption — reduces waste
Prevents die over-cooling from excess lubricant
Compatible with all ESPON die lubricant grades
