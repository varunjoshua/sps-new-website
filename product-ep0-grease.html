EP0 GREASE
EP0 Extreme Pressure Grease (NLGI Grade 0)
EP0 GREASE is a semi-fluid lithium-based grease with extreme pressure (EP) additives, formulated for the centralised lubrication systems of hot forging presses and rolling mills. Its Grade 0 consistency makes it easily pumpable through long delivery lines to remote bearing points, while its EP additives protect bearings under the high loads encountered in these operations.

Key Benefits

Easily pumpable through long centralised systems
Stable viscosity at 100–150°C operating temperature
EP additives protect bearings under high load
Prevents bearing failure and system choking
Eliminates overflow and fire hazard from light oils
Compatible with standard centralised pump units

Applications

Hot Forging PressesRolling MillsCentralised LubricationHammer Bearings
