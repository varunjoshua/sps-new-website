EP00 GREASE
EP00 Extreme Pressure Grease (NLGI Grade 00)
EP00 GREASE is the ultra-fluid variant in the ESPON EP range, designed for centralised lubrication systems that cycle at high frequency or where pump delivery distances are longest. Its Grade 00 consistency ensures consistent flow even in cold-start conditions, while its thermal stability prevents oxidation and breakdown at the elevated ambient temperatures around forging and rolling equipment.

Key Benefits

Ultra-fluid for high-frequency cycling systems
Consistent flow in cold-start conditions
Thermally stable at elevated ambient temperatures
Prevents bearing seizing in high-speed equipment
Long service intervals — reduced top-up frequency
Suitable for multi-line centralised pump systems
