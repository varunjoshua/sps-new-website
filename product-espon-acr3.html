ESPON-ACR3 Advanced Anti-Scale Coating

High-performance anti-scale coating for superalloys, titanium alloys, and high-alloy steels. Provides superior oxidation protection at extreme temperatures with enhanced adhesion on difficult-to-coat surfaces.

ESPON-ACR3
Advanced Anti-Scale Coating for High-Alloy Steels
ESPON-ACR3 is the high-performance variant of the ESPON anti-scale range, specifically developed for superalloys, titanium alloys, and high-chromium steels that require extra protection against oxidation and contamination during elevated-temperature processing. Its advanced binder system ensures reliable adhesion even on reactive alloy surfaces.

Key Benefits

Superior oxidation protection for superalloys
Prevents surface contamination of titanium alloys
Enhanced adhesion on reactive alloy surfaces
Reduces alpha case formation in titanium
Clean removal after processing — no residue
Compatible with vacuum and atmosphere furnaces
