ESPON-AS Anti-Scale Coating

General-purpose anti-scale coating for forging billets in gas and oil-fired furnaces. Suitable for open-die and closed-die forging operations of carbon and alloy steels.

ESPON-AS
Anti-Scale Coating for Hot Forging
ESPON-AS is applied to billet surfaces prior to furnace loading, forming a protective layer that limits oxidation during soaking and transfer to the forging press. It reduces flash formation, improves forging fill, and decreases die wear caused by scale acting as an abrasive between die and workpiece.

Key Benefits

Reduces scale formation on forging billets
Decreases die wear caused by scale abrasion
Improves die fill and forging accuracy
Compatible with gas-fired and oil-fired furnaces
Easy spray or brush application on cold billets
No toxic or hazardous constituents

Applications

Open-Die Forging Closed-Die Forging Carbon Steel Billets Alloy Steel Billets Gas-Fired Furnaces Oil-Fired Furnaces
