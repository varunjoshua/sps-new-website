ESPON-DFK3 Heavy Forging Die Lubricant

High-film-strength die lubricant for heavy closed-die forging of large components. Withstands high impact loads and extreme die surface temperatures.

ESPON-DFK3
High-Film-Strength Lubricant for Heavy Forgings
ESPON-DFK3 is formulated for the most demanding closed-die forging operations — large automotive, aerospace, and industrial components where die surface temperatures exceed 300°C and impact loads are at their highest. Its enhanced film-strength additives ensure the lubricant film does not break down between strokes, protecting die faces and maintaining consistent forging quality.

Key Benefits

Exceptional film strength under high impact loads
Maintains film integrity above 300°C die temperature
Reduces soldering and welding of forging to die
Extends die life in heavy closed-die operations
Suitable for complex large-section forgings
Reduced smoke generation vs. conventional lubricants
