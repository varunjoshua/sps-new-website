ESPON-DLO3 Advanced Oil-Based Die Lubricant

Third-generation oil-based die lubricant with improved additive package for enhanced die protection, reduced smoke, and better forging surface finish.

ESPON-DLO3
Advanced Oil-Based Die Lubricant (3rd Generation)
ESPON-DLO3 builds on the proven ESPON-DLO formulation with an enhanced additive package that significantly reduces smoke generation, improves die surface finish on the forged component, and extends die life further. Its third-generation chemistry delivers better lubrication per gram of product applied, making it more economical in use despite a higher initial cost.

Key Benefits

Significantly reduced smoke generation vs. DLO
Improved surface finish on forged components
Extended die life vs. first-generation oil lubricants
Better lubrication efficiency — less lubricant per stroke
Reduced worker exposure to lubricant smoke and fumes
Compatible with automated spray systems

Applications

Oil-Fired Press LinesHigh-Temp Die ForgingStainless & Tool SteelsExtrusion Presses
