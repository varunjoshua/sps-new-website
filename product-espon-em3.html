ESPON-EM3 Emulsion Die Lubricant

Emulsion-based die lubricant for medium to heavy forging operations. Stable, adherent film at elevated die temperatures with excellent release properties.

ESPON-EM3
Emulsion Die Lubricant for Medium-Heavy Forging
ESPON-EM3 forms a stable emulsion with water that, when applied to hot dies, leaves a uniform lubricating film that provides both release and thermal barrier properties. It is particularly effective on dies with complex cavities where film stability at elevated temperatures is critical for consistent forging fill and die life.

Key Benefits

Stable emulsion — consistent film formation on hot dies
Excellent lubricity in complex die cavities
Thermal barrier effect reduces die surface temperature
Improved forging fill and dimensional accuracy
Compatible with SPS spray systems
Water-based — no solvent or graphite
