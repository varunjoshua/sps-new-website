O-CLEAN3 Oil Cleaning Liquid

Concentrated water-based degreaser for industrial forge and rolling shop environments. Removes oil, grease, and accumulated grime from floors, machinery, and equipment.

O-CLEAN3
Industrial Degreaser for Forge Shops
O-CLEAN3 is a concentrated alkaline cleaning solution that emulsifies and lifts heavy oil deposits, graphite residue, and mixed oil-mud cake from all surfaces in the forge and rolling shop. Diluted with water, it is safe on metal, painted surfaces, plastic trays, and skin — replacing hazardous solvent-based cleaners without compromising cleaning effectiveness.
