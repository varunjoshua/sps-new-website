Anti-Nitriding Stop-Off Paste

Selective masking pastes for gas nitriding and plasma-ion nitriding processes — protecting surfaces where nitriding must be prevented, such as threads, bores, and mating faces.

ESPON anti-nitriding pastes are applied to surfaces where nitrogen penetration must be prevented during the nitriding cycle. After the process, the paste is easily removed, leaving clean, un-nitrided surfaces ready for machining or assembly. This significantly extends die service life and reduces post-process machining time.
