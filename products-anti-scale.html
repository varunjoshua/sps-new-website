Anti-Scale Protective Coatings

Water-based coatings that prevent oxidation and decarburisation of steel billets, ingots, and bars during furnace heating — recovering 1–8% metal loss back to near-zero.

All ESPON anti-scale coatings are applied to billet or ingot surfaces before furnace entry. They form a protective barrier that prevents atmospheric oxygen and nitrogen from reacting with the steel surface during high-temperature soaking — significantly reducing scale formation, metal loss, and decarburisation.
