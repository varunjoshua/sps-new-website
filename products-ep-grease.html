ESPON EP Grease

Extreme-pressure lithium-based greases for centralised lubrication systems of hot forging presses, rolling mills, and heavy industrial machinery operating at elevated temperatures.

ESPON EP greases are engineered to maintain viscosity stability in the temperature ranges encountered by centralised lubrication systems on hot forging and hot rolling equipment. Unlike conventional greases that thin out or oxidise at elevated temperatures, ESPON EP grades prevent system choking, bearing failure, and fire hazards caused by grease overflow onto hot surfaces.
