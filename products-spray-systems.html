Die Lubricant Spray Systems

Precision spray systems and guns engineered for correct die lubricant application — ensuring uniform die coverage, optimal die temperature control, and minimum lubricant wastage.

The performance of any die lubricant is only as good as how it is applied. SPS spray systems are designed to work in conjunction with ESPON die lubricants, delivering the right spray pressure, droplet size, and coverage pattern for each die geometry and press type. Correct application prevents die over-heating, over-cooling, and excess lubricant waste.
